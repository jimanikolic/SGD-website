About Me

Hello!

My name is Jim and I am a currently enrolled senior at the University of Kentucky. I am a Lewis Honor's student graduating in the spring 2024 semester with a bachelors in Computer Science and a minor in math.

I am also a pre-dental student and I am pleased to say that after graduation I am matriculating into the University of Kentucky College of Dentistry class of 2028!

I have experience tutoring at both the college and high-school level through volunteering at the Carnegie Center for Literacy & Learning and tutoring my peers on campus.

I love tutoring because it's a chance to connect with others and share the joy of learning. Being part of someone's educational journey is incredibly rewarding, as I get to witness students grow into more confident, curious individuals. It's not just about teaching; it's about building meaningful connections and helping people discover their own unique path to success.

I look forward to meeting you!
